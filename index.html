Frequently Asked Questions

Is Stylo AI completely free to use?

Yes, Stylo AI offers a robust Free Forever plan. You can generate unlimited drafts, use the visual editor, and deploy up to 3 live websites on our subdomains without ever entering a credit card. We believe in letting you build and experiment without barriers. For advanced features like code export and team collaboration, you can upgrade to our Pro plans.

Do I need coding skills to build a website?

Not at all! Stylo AI is designed for everyone. You can build complex, functional websites simply by describing them in plain English (e.g., "Make a portfolio for a photographer"). However, if you are a developer, our built-in Monaco-based code editor and "Ask AI" debugging tools give you granular control to tweak every line of HTML, CSS, and JS manually.

Can I export the code and host it elsewhere?

Absolutely. Unlike other website builders that lock you into their ecosystem, Stylo AI believes in code freedom. With the Pro plan, you can export clean, semantic, and production-ready HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files. You can then host these files on Netlify, Vercel, AWS, or your own private server.

Who owns the intellectual property of the site?

You maintain 100% ownership. Any website, code, or asset you generate within Stylo AI belongs entirely to you. You are free to use your creations for personal portfolios, commercial business sites, or even client work without any attribution requirements or royalties.

What if I am not satisfied with the generated design?

Stylo AI excels at iteration. If the first result isn't perfect, simply keep chatting with the AI to refine itâ€”change colors, swap layouts, rewrite text, or add new sections instantly. You can also use the "Visual Editor" to drag and drop elements manually, ensuring the final result matches your exact vision.

How does the hosting and deployment work?

We provide instant, one-click deployment. Your sites are hosted on a global CDN (Content Delivery Network) ensuring lightning-fast load times and 99.9% uptime. Every deployed site automatically receives a free SSL certificate (HTTPS) to ensure your visitors' security.
